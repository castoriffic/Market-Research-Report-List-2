<p><p>The Fiber Delivered Direct Diode Lasers market can be categorized into three types based on power levels: low power, mid power, and high power. Low power direct diode lasers typically have power outputs ranging from a few milliwatts to several watts, making them suitable for applications such as laser pointers and barcode scanners. Mid power lasers fall in the range of a few watts to a few hundred watts, making them ideal for tasks like material processing and surgical procedures. High power lasers, on the other hand, have outputs in the kilowatt range, finding utility in industrial cutting, welding, and additive manufacturing processes.</p></p>
