<p><p>The printed and flexible electronics market encompasses various types of printing technologies. Screen printers use a mesh to transfer ink onto a substrate. Inkjet printers spray tiny droplets of ink onto a surface. Aerosol jet printers create patterns by depositing tiny droplets of ink through a nozzle. Flexographic printers use flexible plates to transfer ink onto materials. Gravure printers use engraved cylinders to apply ink. Coating systems apply a thin layer of material onto a surface. Nano imprinting and embossing create patterns by pressing materials onto a substrate. Transfer printers transfer ink from a carrier to a surface. Offset printers use plates and rubber blankets for ink transfer.</p></p>
