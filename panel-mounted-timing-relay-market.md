<p><strong>The Panel Mounted Timing Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-delay</li><li>Off-delay</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Panel Mounted Timing Relay market includes different types such as on-delay, off-delay, and others. On-delay timing relays are designed to delay the activation of an output circuit after the input signal is received. Off-delay timing relays, on the other hand, delay the deactivation of the output circuit after the input signal is removed. The "others" category typically includes various timing relay types that do not fall into the on-delay or off-delay categories, such as interval timing relays, repeat cycle timing relays, and programmable timing relays.</p></p>
