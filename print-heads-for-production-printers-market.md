<p><p>Print heads for production printers are essential components that determine the quality and efficiency of the printing process. There are two main market types of print heads: piezoelectric print heads and thermal print heads. Piezoelectric print heads work by applying electric current to piezoelectric elements, which deform and generate pressure to eject ink droplets onto paper. On the other hand, thermal print heads utilize heat to generate bubbles, which propel ink onto the paper. Both print head types offer distinct advantages and are utilized in various production printing applications.</p></p>
