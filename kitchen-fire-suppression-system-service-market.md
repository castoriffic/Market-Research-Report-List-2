<p><strong>The Kitchen Fire Suppression System Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Chemistry Based</li><li>Water Mist Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen fire suppression system service market can be categorized into two types: wet chemistry based and water mist based. Wet chemistry based systems use a special extinguishing agent specifically designed to control fires in commercial kitchens caused by oil, grease, and solid fuels. These systems provide quick, effective suppression by creating a barrier between the fuel and the oxygen needed for the fire. On the other hand, water mist based systems use fine water droplets to suppress fires by cooling the flames and creating a mist curtain that prevents the fire from spreading. These systems are suitable for various types of fires including those caused by cooking oils and solid fuels. Both types of systems play a crucial role in preventing and controlling kitchen fires.</p></p>
