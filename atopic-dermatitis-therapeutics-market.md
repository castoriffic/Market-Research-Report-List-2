<p><p>Atopic dermatitis therapeutics market includes two main types of treatment methods: topical agents and systemic therapies. They can include creams, ointments, and lotions that help to relieve symptoms such as itching and inflammation. These therapies are typically used for more severe cases of atopic dermatitis.</p></p>
