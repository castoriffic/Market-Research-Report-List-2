<p><p>The Embolectomy Balloon Catheter market includes two types: Single Lumen and Double Lumen. Single Lumen catheters consist of a single tube used to extract blood clots from blocked blood vessels. These devices are commonly used in smaller vessels. On the other hand, Double Lumen catheters have two tubes; one is used to remove the blood clot while the other delivers medication or contrast agents. This type is commonly employed in larger vessels or when additional therapies are needed during the procedure.</p></p>
