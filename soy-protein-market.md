<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Soy protein market comprises three main types: concentrated soy protein, isolated soy protein, and textured soy protein. Concentrated soy protein is derived by removing the fiber and carbohydrates from soybeans, resulting in a higher protein content. Isolated soy protein undergoes further processing to remove additional non-protein components, leaving behind a protein concentrate with minimal fat or carbohydrates. Textured soy protein is made by processing defatted soy flour or soy protein isolate to create a fibrous, meat-like texture. Each type serves various uses and applications across the food industry.</p></p>
