<p><ul><li>Machine-Tufted Carpets</li><li>Hand-Tufted Carpets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tufted carpets are available in two main types: machine-tufted carpets and hand-tufted carpets. Machine-tufted carpets are produced using automated machines that insert pre-dyed yarns into a backing material to create the carpet pile. These carpets are efficiently produced and are commonly used in commercial settings. On the other hand, hand-tufted carpets are created by skilled artisans who manually insert yarns into a backing material using a handheld tool. These carpets are generally considered more luxurious and are often used in residential settings.</p></p>
