<p><p>The Spiral Wound Gaskets market encompasses various types, including Style CG, Style CGI, Style R, and Style RIR. Style CG gaskets are designed for use in raised face flanges, providing a tight seal and preventing leakage. Style CGI gaskets feature an inner ring to enhance sealing capability while maintaining the structural integrity of the outer windings. Style R gaskets are mainly used in male and female flanges, delivering efficient sealing performance. Style RIR gaskets combine the characteristics of both Style R and Style CGI, providing superior sealing in flanges with male and female facing.</p></p>
