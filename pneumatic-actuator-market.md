<p><p>Single-acting pneumatic actuators are devices that generate motion in only one direction, typically using air pressure to extend or retract a piston. These actuators are commonly used in applications where the force is required in one direction only.</p><p>Quarter-turn actuators are a type of pneumatic actuator that are specifically designed to generate rotary motion of 90 degrees (a quarter turn). They are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, oil and gas, and water treatment, where valves or other equipment require precise quarter-turn operations. These actuators offer efficient and reliable control over the movement of valves or other rotary equipment.</p></p>
