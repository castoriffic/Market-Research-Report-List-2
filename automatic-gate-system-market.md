<p><p>The automatic gate system market offers various types of control options for access. Keypad control involves entering a specific code to open the gate. Remote control allows opening and closing of the gate through a handheld device. Voice recognition enables gate access by recognizing specific spoken commands. Face identification utilizes facial recognition technology to grant access. Iris scan involves scanning the unique pattern of the iris for authentication. Telephone access allows gate opening through a phone call. Lastly, other options exist, such as proximity cards or sensors, for accessing the gate by detecting nearby devices or movement.</p></p>
