<p><p>Plasma atomized metallic powders are fine particles of different metal alloys that are produced through the process of plasma atomization. This process involves heating and melting the raw material using plasma torches, and then rapidly cooling and solidifying the molten metal droplets into powder form. The resulting powders have a spherical shape, narrow particle size distribution, and high purity, making them suitable for various applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, electronics, and healthcare.</p><p>The global plasma atomized metallic powders market is expected to witness steady growth in the forecast period.
