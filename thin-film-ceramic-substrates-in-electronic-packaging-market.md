<p><p>Thin film ceramic substrates are materials used in electronic packaging to provide a foundation for electronic components. Alumina thin film ceramic substrates are made from aluminum oxide and offer excellent thermal conductivity and electrical insulation. They are commonly used in high-power applications. On the other hand, AlN thin film ceramic substrates are made from aluminum nitride and have superior thermal conductivity. They are ideal for electronic packaging that requires efficient heat dissipation, such as in power electronics and LED applications.</p></p>
