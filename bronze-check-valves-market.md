<p><p>The Bronze Check Valves market consists of two types of end connections: Solder End Connections and Threaded End Connections. Solder End Connections refer to valves that are soldered onto pipes, ensuring a secure and leak-proof connection. On the other hand, Threaded End Connections are designed with threads that enable them to be screwed onto the pipe, providing a reliable connection. These types of connections are commonly used in various industries that require the prevention of backflow or the regulation of fluid flow.</p></p>
