<p><ul><li>Single Controller</li><li>Dual Source Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Fire Pump Controllers market includes two main types: Single Controller and Dual Source Controller. Single Controller refers to a system where only one controller operates the electric fire pump. It is commonly used in situations where a single power source is sufficient for powering the fire pump. On the other hand, Dual Source Controller involves two controllers, each connected to a separate power source. This type of controller provides a backup power option in case one power source fails, ensuring continuous operation of the fire pump.</p></p>
