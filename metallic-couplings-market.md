<p><p>The metallic couplings market includes different types, such as chain couplings and gear couplings, both serving the purpose of connecting two shafts to transmit power. Chain couplings consist of interconnected links forming a chain, providing flexibility and compensating for misalignments. On the other hand, gear couplings use toothed gears to transmit torque while maintaining constant speed and alignment. Both types of couplings are widely used in various industries to ensure efficient power transmission and minimize wear and tear on connected machinery.</p></p>
