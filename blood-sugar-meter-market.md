<p><strong>The Blood Sugar Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood sugar meter market consists of two types: standard and continuous. Standard blood sugar meters are the traditional devices used for self-monitoring blood glucose levels. They require the user to prick their finger and place a drop of blood on a test strip for measurement. Continuous blood sugar meters, on the other hand, provide real-time glucose readings throughout the day by using a small sensor implanted under the skin. They eliminate the need for frequent finger pricks and provide more convenience and continuous monitoring for individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
