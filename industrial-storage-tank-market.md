<p><p>The Industrial Storage Tank market includes two types - Pressurized and Non-Pressurized tanks. Pressurized tanks are designed to store materials under pressure, ensuring no leakage or external contamination. These tanks are often used for storing gases or liquids that require high pressure for transportation or specific industrial processes. On the other hand, Non-Pressurized tanks are used to store materials that do not need to be kept under pressure. These tanks are usually designed to store large quantities of non-hazardous substances, such as water, oil, or chemicals.</p></p>
