<p><p>Fortified edible oils market offers various types of oils including palm oil, soybean oil, sunflower oil, olive oil, corn oil, canola oil, rice bran oil, and other oils. Palm oil, derived from the fruit of oil palm trees, is high in saturated fat and commonly used in cooking and food products. Soybean oil, extracted from soybeans, is popular due to its high nutritional value. Sunflower oil, made from sunflower seeds, is known for its light flavor and high smoke point. Olive oil, obtained from olives, is renowned for its health benefits and rich taste. Corn oil, derived from corn kernels, is suitable for frying and baking. Canola oil, made from canola plant seeds, is low in saturated fat and commonly used for cooking. Rice bran oil, extracted from rice bran, offers a high smoke point and is used in Asian cuisine. Other fortified edible oils include various types like safflower oil, grapeseed oil, avocado oil, etc., which have their unique characteristics and culinary applications in different parts of the world.</p></p>
