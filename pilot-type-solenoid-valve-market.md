<p><p>The Pilot-Type Solenoid Valve market can be categorized into different types based on their functionality. Two-way SV refers to valves that have two ports - an inlet and an outlet - allowing the flow of fluid in one direction. Three-way SV has an additional port, typically used for diverting the flow of fluid. Four-way SV includes four ports and is commonly used in applications where the fluid flow needs to be stopped, split, or mixed in different directions. Lastly, Others market includes different types of solenoid valves that do not fall into the above categories.</p></p>
