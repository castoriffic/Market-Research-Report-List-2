<p><p>The portable ultrasound machine market encompasses various modes such as A-mode, B-mode or 2D mode, C-mode, M-mode, Doppler mode, pulse inversion mode, and harmonic mode. A-mode displays signal amplitude, B-mode displays 2D images, C-mode shows cross-sectional images, M-mode records images in motion, Doppler mode captures blood flow, pulse inversion mode enhances tissue contrast, and harmonic mode improves image quality by reducing artifacts. These different modes cater to specific diagnostic needs and contribute to the growth and diversity of the portable ultrasound machine market.</p></p>
