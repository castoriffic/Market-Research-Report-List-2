<p><p>The bench grinder market encompasses two main types: benchtop and handheld. Benchtop grinders are designed to be installed on workbenches or tables, offering stability and better control while sharpening or grinding materials. These grinders typically have a larger wheel size and more powerful motors suitable for heavy-duty tasks. On the other hand, handheld grinders are portable and can be carried around for on-site use. They are smaller in size and often feature a smaller wheel, making them suitable for lighter grinding and sharpening jobs in various locations.</p></p>
