<p><p>The ceramic tile cutter market is divided into two types: wet tile cutters and dry tile cutters. Wet tile cutters are equipped with a water reservoir that continuously sprays water on the cutting area to cool down the blade and reduce dust, resulting in clean and precise cuts. On the other hand, dry tile cutters do not use water and rely on a diamond-tipped blade to cut tiles smoothly with minimal dust. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, offering options for professionals and DIY enthusiasts alike.</p></p>
