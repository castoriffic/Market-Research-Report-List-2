<p><p>Luxury essential oils are categorized into two types - compound essential oil and unilateral essential oil. Compound essential oils are blends of multiple essential oils, carefully formulated to create unique and complex aromas. They offer a wide range of benefits due to the combined properties of different oils. On the other hand, unilateral essential oils are single oils derived from a specific plant or herb, offering the pure and concentrated essence of that particular plant. They are favored for their simplicity and targeted therapeutic properties. Both types cater to the growing demand for high-quality aromatherapy and personal care products.</p></p>
