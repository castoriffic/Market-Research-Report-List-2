<p><p>KPI software market encompasses two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based KPI software refers to applications hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of implementation with minimal upfront costs. On the other hand, on-premises KPI software requires installation on local servers and infrastructure. It provides greater control over data and security but demands more significant upfront investments and ongoing maintenance.
