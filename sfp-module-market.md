<p><strong>The SFP Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Singlemode SFP Module</li><li>Multimode SFP Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SFP module market encompasses two main types: singlemode SFP modules and multimode SFP modules. Singlemode SFP modules are designed for long-distance communication over singlemode fiber optic cables, typically used for telecommunications and larger network deployments. In contrast, multimode SFP modules are used for shorter distances and are often employed in data centers and LAN environments. Both types of SFP modules enable the transfer of data, voice, and video signals, but differ in terms of distance coverage and their respective areas of application.</p></p>
