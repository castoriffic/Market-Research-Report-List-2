<p><p>Industrial controls are systems used to oversee and manage industrial processes. The different types of industrial control systems include Distributed Control System (DCS), which autonomously manages and monitors individual units within a plant. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition System (SCADA) enables centralized control and monitoring of various industrial processes. Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) facilitate real-time production monitoring and coordination. Lastly, the "Others" segment includes lesser-known industrial control systems catering to specific requirements. These systems collectively streamline operations, improve productivity, and enhance safety in industrial sectors.</p></p>
