<p><p>Radiation processing for the food industry involves using gamma or accelerator based technology to enhance food safety and quality. Gamma based market refers to the use of gamma rays emitted from a radioactive isotope, such as cobalt-60, to treat food products. This method effectively kills harmful bacteria and insects, extending shelf life. Accelerator based market, on the other hand, utilizes electron beams generated by electron accelerators to achieve similar results. Both approaches have their advantages and are widely adopted in the food industry to prevent spoilage, reduce chemical treatments, and ensure food security.</p></p>
