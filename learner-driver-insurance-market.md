<p><ul><li>Own Car</li><li>Someone else's Car</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Learner driver insurance is available in two market types: Own Car and Someone else's Car. Own Car insurance covers learners who want to practice driving in their own vehicle under supervision. This type of insurance provides coverage for the learner driver and the car they own. On the other hand, Someone else's Car insurance allows learners to practice in someone else's car, like a family member's or friend's vehicle. This insurance type provides coverage for the learner driver while they are driving another person's car with their permission.</p></p>
