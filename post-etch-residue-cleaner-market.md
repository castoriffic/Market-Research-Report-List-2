<p><p>The Post Etch Residue Cleaner market comprises two main types: Aqueous and Semi-aqueous solutions. They are environmentally friendly, cost-effective, and widely used in various industries. On the other hand, Semi-aqueous cleaners are a blend of water and organic solvents.
