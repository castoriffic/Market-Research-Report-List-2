<p><p>Composite doors and windows are made from two or more materials that are combined to create a stronger and more durable product. There are two types of composite materials commonly used in the market: Fiber Reinforced Plastics (FRP) and Wood Plastic Composites (WPC). FRP composites are made by reinforcing plastics with fibers such as fiberglass to enhance their strength and stiffness. On the other hand, WPC composites are a combination of wood fibers and thermoplastics, creating a material that has the appearance of wood but with added durability and resistance to rot and insects.</p></p>
