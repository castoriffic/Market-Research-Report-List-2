On the other hand, Bluetooth tracking devices use short-range wireless technology to connect with a smartphone or another device. Bluetooth devices are usually smaller and do not rely on satellite signals, making them more suitable for indoor tracking or short-distance monitoring. Both GPS and Bluetooth devices aim to enhance personal safety by keeping track of individuals' movements and location.</p></p>
