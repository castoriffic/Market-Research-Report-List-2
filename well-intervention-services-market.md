<p><ul><li>Light Intervention</li><li>Heavy Intervention</li><li>Medium Intervention</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Well intervention services are categorized into three types: light intervention, heavy intervention, and medium intervention. Light intervention involves minor tasks, such as wireline work, slickline work, or basic well maintenance. Heavy intervention is required for extensive operations, like wellhead maintenance, complex well plug and abandonment, or major well repairs. Medium intervention falls in between, performing tasks that are more involved than light intervention but not as extensive as heavy intervention. These different types cater to varying well intervention needs, providing targeted services depending on the complexity and magnitude of the tasks at hand.</p></p>
