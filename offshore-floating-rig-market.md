<p><strong>The Offshore Floating Rig Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jackup</li><li>Semi-Submersibles</li><li>Drillships</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore floating rig market consists of three major types: jackups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackup rigs are typically used in shallow waters and are equipped with legs that can be lowered to the seabed for stability. Semi-submersibles are capable of floating on water surface and partially submerging their hulls for stability during drilling operations. Drillships are vessels that can drill in deepwater locations and are equipped with a dynamic positioning system to maintain their position. Each rig type has specific advantages and is suitable for different offshore drilling scenarios.</p></p>
