<p><p>The Hall Current Sensor market is divided into two types: Analog Output Hall Sensor and Digital Output Hall Sensor. The Analog Output Hall Sensor provides a continuous analog signal proportional to the current being measured, allowing for precise and real-time measurements. On the other hand, the Digital Output Hall Sensor converts the current to a digital signal, which provides more accurate and reliable measurements. Both types cater to different requirements and applications in industries such as power systems, automotive, and industrial automation.</p></p>
