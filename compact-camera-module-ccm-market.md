<p><p>Compact Camera Module (CCM) is a type of camera module used in various devices, such as smartphones and tablets, to capture images and videos. There are two types of CCMs commonly used: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD utilizes a grid of light sensors to convert light into electrical signals, providing high-quality images. CMOS, on the other hand, uses an array of active pixels to capture and convert light. CMOS is more popular due to its lower power consumption, faster readout speed, and better integration with other components.</p></p>
