<p><p>The disposable repositioning sling market consists of three main types: polyester, spacer, and others. Polyester slings are widely used due to their durability, high strength, and resistance to tearing and abrasion. Spacer slings feature a unique design that allows air circulation, reducing the risk of skin maceration and pressure ulcers. Lastly, the "others" category includes slings made from alternative materials or with distinct features, such as anti-microbial properties or special padding. These different types aim to meet the diverse needs of healthcare facilities and ensure patient comfort and safety during repositioning procedures.</p></p>
