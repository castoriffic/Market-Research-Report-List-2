<p><p>The Cloud Contact Center Software market is categorized into three types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. In the Public Cloud market, the contact center software is hosted on a third-party provider's infrastructure accessible to everyone over the internet. Private Cloud market refers to a contact center software deployed within an organization's own infrastructure, ensuring complete data control and security. Hybrid Cloud market combines both Public and Private Cloud, allowing organizations to have a flexible and customizable solution while accessing additional resources from the Public Cloud when needed.</p></p>
