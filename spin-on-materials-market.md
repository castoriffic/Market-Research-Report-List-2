Spin-on Metal Oxide Hardmasks (MHM) are similar materials, but composed of metal oxides. Both SOC and MHM provide a protective layer during etching processes. The "Others" category includes various additional spin-on materials used for applications like anti-reflective coatings, planarization, and dielectric insulation, among others. These materials enhance the performance and functionality of electronic devices.</p></p>
