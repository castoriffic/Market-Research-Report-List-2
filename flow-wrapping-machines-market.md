<p><p>Flow wrapping machines are used for packaging products by forming a seal around them. There are two main types of flow wrapping machines: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal flow wrappers are designed for packaging products that are long, flat, or irregularly shaped, such as cookies or sandwiches. They utilize a horizontal sealing mechanism. On the other hand, vertical flow wrappers are suitable for packaging items that are solid, regular in shape, and need a vertical seal, such as candy bars or soap bars. Each type of machine caters to specific packaging needs and product characteristics.</p></p>
