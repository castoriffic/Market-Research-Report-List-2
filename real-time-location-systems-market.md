<p><p>Real Time Location Systems (RTLS) are used to track the real-time location of people or assets within a specific area. There are several types of RTLS technologies available. RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) uses radio waves to locate and identify tags attached to objects. Wi-Fi-based RTLS utilizes Wi-Fi signals to determine location. Ultrasound RTLS utilizes ultrasonic waves to track objects. Infrared RTLS uses infrared signals for location tracking. ZigBee is a wireless technology that enables communication between devices. Ultra-Wide Band (UWB) is a radio technology that offers precise location tracking. Other types of RTLS technologies include GPS, Bluetooth, and cellular networks.</p></p>
