<p><p>Bakery ingredient market encompasses various types like fortification agents, emulsifiers, functional blends, and others. Fortification agents are additives that enhance the nutritional value of bakery products, such as adding vitamins or minerals. Emulsifiers, on the other hand, are used to stabilize the mixture of water and fats in baked goods, improving their texture and shelf life. Functional blends are a combination of ingredients that provide specific functionalities like improving dough elasticity or enhancing flavor. The "others" category typically includes ingredients like enzymes, preservatives, and leavening agents used in baking processes.</p></p>
