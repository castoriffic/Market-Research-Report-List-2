<p><p>Angle rolls are versatile machines used in metal fabrication to bend various types of angle iron, flat bars, and other structural materials. There are two main types of angle rolls: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical angle rolls use a set of gears to power the bending process, offering durability and precision. Hydraulic angle rolls, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic cylinders to apply pressure, providing higher bending capacities and greater ease of use. Both types cater to different applications, so the choice depends on the specific bending needs and requirements of the user.</p></p>
