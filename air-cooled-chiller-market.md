<p><p>The Air Cooled Chiller market includes two main types: Air Cooled Scroll Chiller and Air Cooled Screw Chiller. Air Cooled Scroll Chillers utilize a scroll compressor to cool the system, offering energy-efficient performance and low noise levels. On the other hand, Air Cooled Screw Chillers use a screw compressor, which provides greater cooling capacity and is suitable for larger applications. Both types offer the advantage of being air-cooled, eliminating the need for water cooling systems and reducing overall operating costs.</p></p>
