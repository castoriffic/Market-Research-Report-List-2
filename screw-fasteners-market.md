<p><p>Screw fasteners are widely used in various industries and can be divided into two main types: external thread fasteners and internal threaded fasteners. External thread fasteners, such as bolts and screws, have threads on their outer surface that serve to tighten and secure objects together. Internal threaded fasteners, such as nuts and threaded inserts, have threads on their inner surface to create a mating connection with external threaded fasteners. Both types play a crucial role in providing strong and durable fastening solutions for a wide range of applications in industries like construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
