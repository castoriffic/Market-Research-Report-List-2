<p><p>A Temperature Compensated Xtal Oscillator (TCXO) is a type of crystal oscillator that is designed to maintain a stable output frequency despite changes in temperature. It achieves this by using a compensation circuit that adjusts the frequency of the oscillator as the temperature changes. TCXOs are commonly used in applications where high accuracy and stability of frequency are required, such as GPS systems, telecommunications equipment, and military applications.</p><p>The current outlook of the TCXO market is positive, with the market expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecasted period.
