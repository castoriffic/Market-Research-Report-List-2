<p><p>Coalescing filters are utilized in various industries to remove liquid and solid contaminants from air or gas streams. Vertical coalescing filters are designed to operate with a vertical flow path, allowing gravity to assist in the separation of contaminants. They are commonly used in applications where space is limited. Conversely, horizontal coalescing filters are designed with a horizontal flow path, providing efficient contaminant separation in larger systems. The choice between vertical and horizontal filters depends on the specific requirements of the application and available space.</p></p>
