<p><strong>The Railway Suspension System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Secondary Suspension Products</li><li>Primary Suspension Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Railway Suspension System market comprises of two main types: Secondary Suspension Products and Primary Suspension Products. Secondary Suspension Products refer to components like air springs, dampers, and shock absorbers that are responsible for providing comfort and stability to passengers by minimizing vibrations and impact. On the other hand, Primary Suspension Products include bogies and wheelsets that support the weight of the train, provide traction, and ensure smooth movement by absorbing unevenness on the tracks. Both these types of suspension products play vital roles in ensuring safe and comfortable railway transportation.</p></p>
