<p><strong>The Optical Coherence Tomography Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time-Domain Type</li><li>Spectral-Domain Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Imaging System market is classified into two types: Time-Domain Type and Spectral-Domain Type. </p><p>The Time-Domain Type uses a low-coherence light source and a scanning mirror to produce images. It captures one depth profile at a time, resulting in slower imaging speed but high axial resolution. </p><p>On the other hand, the Spectral-Domain Type employs a broadband light source and a spectrometer to simultaneously acquire depth profiles. This technology offers faster imaging speed and improved sensitivity, allowing for real-time imaging and enhanced visualization of tissue structures.</p></p>
