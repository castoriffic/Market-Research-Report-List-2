<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Tabletop (Desktop, Benchtop) SEM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>W-SEM</li><li>FEG-SEM</li><li>FIB-SEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tabletop SEM (Scanning Electron Microscope) market consists of three main types: W-SEM, FEG-SEM, and FIB-SEM. W-SEM (Tungsten-SEM) is the most basic tabletop option, offering lower resolution images and limited analytical capabilities. FEG-SEM (Field Emission Gun-SEM) provides higher resolution and more analytical features, enabling advanced imaging and analysis. FIB-SEM (Focused Ion Beam-SEM) incorporates a focused ion beam for milling or modifying samples, making it suitable for materials analysis and microfabrication applications. These different types offer varying levels of resolution and functionality to cater to different research requirements.</p></p>
