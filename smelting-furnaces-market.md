<p><p>Smelting furnaces are used to extract metal from ore through high-temperature processes. There are two main types of smelting furnaces: combustion-type and electric-type. Combustion-type furnaces use fuels like coal or natural gas to generate heat, while electric-type furnaces use electricity to generate heat. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages. Combustion-type furnaces are generally cheaper to operate but produce more emissions, while electric-type furnaces are more environmentally friendly but often have higher operating costs. The choice between the two depends on factors such as cost, environmental impact, and specific requirements of the smelting process.</p></p>
