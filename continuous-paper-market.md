<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous paper is a type of paper used in printers that comes in various forms. In single layer continuous paper, it consists of a single layer of paper that can be used for various printing purposes. On the other hand, multi-layer continuous paper consists of multiple layers, usually carbon copies, where the top layer is used for printing and subsequent layers for copying. Both types offer continuous and uninterrupted printing, with single layer being simpler and multi-layer being useful for creating copies of printed documents.</p></p>
