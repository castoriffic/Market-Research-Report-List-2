<p><strong>The Vehicle Body Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CAN Body Control Modules</li><li>LIN Body Control Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Body Control System Market includes various types of control modules used in vehicles. CAN Body Control Modules are control units that use the CAN (Controller Area Network) protocol to communicate with other vehicle systems. They allow for efficient data exchange and control over various body functions like lighting and power windows. On the other hand, LIN Body Control Modules use the LIN (Local Interconnect Network) protocol and are mostly used for simpler body functions like central locking and climate control. Both types of modules play a crucial role in integrating and controlling different parts of the vehicle's body system.</p></p>
