<p><p>Electric Vehicle Service Equipment (EVSE) market encompasses three types of charging levels: Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3. Level 1 charging is the most basic and utilizes a standard household 120-volt outlet, offering a slower charging speed. Level 2 charging utilizes a 240-volt outlet and provides a faster charging speed, making it suitable for residential and commercial settings. Level 3 charging, also known as DC fast charging, uses high-power chargers to rapidly charge electric vehicles, making it ideal for public locations and long-distance travel due to its even faster charging speed.</p></p>
