<p><p>NVH stands for Noise, Vibration, and Harshness. The primary aim of NVH control is to ensure a comfortable and safe environment for end-users.</p><p>The NVH system includes various components such as isolators, dampers, absorbers, and acoustic materials. These parts and materials are designed to reduce vibrations and noise generated by different mechanical systems. The NVH market is driven by factors such as increasing consumer demand for comfort and luxury in vehicles, stringent noise regulations, and the growing focus on noise pollution control.</p><p>In terms of materials used in NVH, the market is witnessing a shift towards lightweight and eco-friendly options. These materials offer improved damping characteristics, reduced weight, and better environmental performance.</p><p>According to market analysis, the global NVH market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period. The aerospace industry is also projected to drive market growth, as aircraft manufacturers strive to reduce noise and vibrations for passenger comfort.</p><p>The latest trend in the NVH market is the integration of advanced technologies such as active noise control, active vibration control, and sound synthesis techniques. Additionally, the market is witnessing collaborations between NVH material manufacturers and research institutions to develop innovative and sustainable solutions.</p><p>Overall, the NVH market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years, driven by technological advancements, increasing awareness regarding noise pollution, and the demand for enhanced comfort in various industries.</p></p>
