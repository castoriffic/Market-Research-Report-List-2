<p><p>A rotary damper, also known as a rotational viscous damper, is a mechanical device that is used to provide controlled resistance to movement in rotary or rotational applications. As the shaft rotates, the fluid is forced through small orifices, creating resistance and damping the movement of the shaft.</p><p>The future outlook of the rotary damper market is promising.
