<p><p>Stem Cells Cryopreservation Equipments Market includes two main types: Liquid Phase and Vapor Phase. Liquid Phase cryopreservation equipment uses liquid nitrogen to freeze stem cells, maintaining a temperature below -150°C. It provides enhanced cell viability and better preservation efficiency. Vapor Phase cryopreservation equipment, on the other hand, utilizes liquid nitrogen vapor to freeze stem cells, ensuring a temperature below -190°C. It offers improved protection against contamination and lower risk of cross-contamination. Both types of equipment are vital in storing stem cells for research, therapy, and other medical applications.</p></p>
