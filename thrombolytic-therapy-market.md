<p><p>Thrombolytic therapy market includes three main types: drug treatment, surgical treatment, and other treatment options. Drug treatment involves the administration of thrombolytic drugs that dissolve blood clots, promoting blood flow. Surgical treatment involves interventions like angioplasty or thrombectomy where the clot is physically removed. Other treatment options include the use of catheter-directed thrombolysis or mechanical devices to break down the clot. These different approaches aim to effectively treat blood clot-related conditions, such as strokes, pulmonary embolism, deep vein thrombosis, and heart attacks.</p></p>
