<p><p>EPON OLT, or Ethernet Passive Optical Network Optical Line Terminal, is a device used in passive optical network (PON) to connect multiple Optical Network Units (ONUs) to the central office. Different market types for EPON OLTs are determined by the number of ports they have. A 4-port EPON OLT can serve up to 512 subscribers, an 8-port can serve up to 1024 subscribers, a 12-port can serve up to 1536 subscribers, and a 16-port can serve up to 2048 subscribers. These varying port capacities allow network operators to choose the most suitable OLT based on the number of subscribers they need to serve.</p></p>
