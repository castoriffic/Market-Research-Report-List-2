<p><strong>The Automated Refuse Collection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity System</li><li>Full Vacuum System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automated refuse collection system market includes two main types: the gravity system and the full vacuum system. The gravity system utilizes underground containers that rely on gravity to transport waste to a central collection point, reducing the need for trucks and manual labor. On the other hand, the full vacuum system uses a network of underground pipes and powerful vacuum systems to automatically transport waste to a central collection point, completely eliminating the use of trucks. Both systems provide efficient and automated solutions for waste collection.</p></p>
