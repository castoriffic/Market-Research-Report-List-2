<p><p>Artificial blood pumps are medical devices used to increase blood flow in patients with cardiovascular diseases. There are different types of artificial blood pumps available in the market. Volumetric blood pumps work by using a piston or a diaphragm to create pressure and push blood forward. They are efficient and provide accurate control of blood flow. On the other hand, vane-type blood pumps use rotating blades to create blood flow. They are durable and offer consistent flow rates. Both types have their advantages and applications in the medical field.</p></p>
