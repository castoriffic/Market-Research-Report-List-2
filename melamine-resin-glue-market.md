<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The melamine resin glue market is divided into two types: powder and liquid. Powder melamine resin glue is made by blending melamine, formaldehyde, and other additives into a powdered form, which is then mixed with water to create a usable glue. On the other hand, liquid melamine resin glue is already in a liquid form and can be directly applied without any mixing. Both types offer strong bonding properties and are commonly used in woodworking, construction, and industrial applications. The choice between powder and liquid depends on the specific requirements and preferences of users.</p></p>
