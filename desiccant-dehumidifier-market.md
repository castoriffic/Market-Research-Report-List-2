<p><p>Desiccant dehumidifiers are categorized into two types: tower type and rotor type. Tower type dehumidifiers consist of a single desiccant rotor tower that absorbs moisture from the air. This type of dehumidifier is useful in industrial and commercial settings due to its high capacity and effectiveness. On the other hand, rotor type dehumidifiers utilize multiple rotating discs to absorb moisture, allowing for a more compact and versatile design. This type of dehumidifier is commonly used in residential and small commercial applications.</p></p>
