<p><p>The flight tracking market consists of various types, namely ADS-B, FANS, and PFTS. ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast) technology allows aircraft to determine their position via satellite navigation and periodically broadcast it to air traffic control. FANS (Future Air Navigation System) utilizes digital communications to enhance the exchange of data between aircraft and air traffic control, improving efficiency and safety. PFTS (Portable Flight Tracking System) enables real-time tracking and monitoring of aircraft using portable devices. These technologies play a crucial role in improving flight safety, optimizing air traffic management, and enhancing overall operational efficiency in the aviation industry.</p></p>
