<p><p>The stationary cycles market can be categorized into three main types - magnetic stationary cycle, electromagnetic stationary cycle, and self-generation stationary cycle. Magnetic stationary cycles use magnets to provide resistance and create a smooth pedaling experience. Electromagnetic stationary cycles utilize electromagnetic force to generate resistance, offering a more precise control over the intensity levels. Self-generation stationary cycles produce electricity while being pedaled, which can be harnessed for powering the equipment or stored for later use. These different types cater to varying preferences and fitness needs of consumers in the market.</p></p>
