<p><p>IC trays, also known as integrated circuit trays, are plastic packaging components used to store and transport semiconductor integrated circuits (ICs). <p><p>The IC trays market consists of various types such as MPPE, PES, PS, ABS, and Others. MPPE trays are made from modified polyphenylene ether material, known for its excellent strength and temperature resistance. PES trays are manufactured using polyethersulfone, providing good chemical resistance and dimensional stability. PS trays are made from polystyrene, commonly used due to their lightweight nature and affordability. ABS trays are composed of acrylonitrile butadiene styrene, known for its high impact strength and durability. Other types of trays include those made from different materials not specifically mentioned.</p></p>
