<p><p>The aviation seat belts market can be classified into different types based on the design and functionality of the seat belts. Two-point seat belts consist of a lap belt that fastens across the passenger's waist. Three-point seat belts include a lap belt and a shoulder belt that fastens across the passenger's waist and shoulder. Four-point seat belts add an additional strap that fastens across the passenger's legs. "Others" refer to any seat belt types not specifically categorized, such as five-point seat belts or specialized restraints. These different types cater to varying safety needs and requirements in aviation.</p></p>
