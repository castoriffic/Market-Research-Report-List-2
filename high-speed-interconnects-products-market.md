<p><p>The high-speed interconnects products market includes two main types: Active Optical Cable (AOC) and Direct Attach Cables (DAC). AOC utilizes optical fibers and optical transceivers to transmit data, providing a longer reach and better signal quality compared to traditional copper cables. DAC, on the other hand, uses copper conductors and transceivers to establish a direct link between devices, offering a cost-effective solution for shorter distances. Both AOC and DAC play significant roles in enhancing data transmission speeds and performance in various industries like telecommunications, data centers, and enterprise networks.</p></p>
