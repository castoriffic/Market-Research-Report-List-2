<p><p>The hollow shaft servo actuator market consists of two main types: hollow shaft rotary servo actuators and hollow shaft linear servo actuators. Hollow shaft rotary servo actuators are designed to provide rotational motion with the shaft passing through the center, allowing for easy integration with other systems. On the other hand, hollow shaft linear servo actuators are designed to provide linear motion along the shaft, enabling precise positioning in applications where linear movement is required. Both types of servo actuators offer enhanced control and accuracy in a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
