<p><p>Industrial clean rooms are specialized environments used in manufacturing and scientific industries to maintain high levels of cleanliness and control airborne contaminants. Turbulently ventilated clean rooms use a combination of airflow and filtration systems to remove contaminants, ensuring a consistent clean environment. In contrast, unidirectional flow clean rooms employ a unidirectional air flow pattern that moves contaminants away from the critical work area. Both types of clean rooms are essential for industries that require strict cleanliness standards to ensure product quality and safety.</p></p>
