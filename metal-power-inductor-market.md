<p><p>The Metal Power Inductor Market comprises two main types: Thin Film Inductors and Wire Wound Inductors. Thin Film Inductors are made using thin layers of conductive material, resulting in smaller sizes and higher frequencies. They are commonly used in applications where space is limited. On the other hand, Wire Wound Inductors are constructed by winding wire around a core, allowing for higher inductance values and better current handling capabilities. They are commonly used in applications requiring higher power levels and lower frequencies.</p></p>
