<p><p>Centrifuge tubes come in various types based on their bottom base design. </p><p>Conical base tubes have a tapered bottom that allows for separation of liquids and solids during centrifugation. This design is commonly used in DNA and protein isolation.</p><p>Round bottom base tubes have a curved bottom that enhances mixing and stirring of liquids during centrifugation. They are suitable for various laboratory applications like cell culture and enzymatic reactions.</p><p>Flat bottom base tubes have a flat bottom surface, ideal for processing large volumes and for sedimentation purposes. They are commonly used for general laboratory tasks and are available in different sizes and materials for different applications.</p></p>
