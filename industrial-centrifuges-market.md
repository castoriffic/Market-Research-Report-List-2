<p><p>Industrial centrifuges are machines used in various industries to separate solids and liquids. Sedimentation centrifuges work based on the principle of gravity, where the denser particles settle at the bottom, allowing for easy separation. Filtering centrifuges, on the other hand, use a filter medium to separate the solids from the liquid. Both types of centrifuges are widely utilized in industries such as chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing, allowing for efficient separation of materials and improved product quality.</p></p>
