Handheld fluorometers are portable devices used for on-site measurements of chlorophyll fluorescence in plants. They are widely used in field research and monitoring applications. On the other hand, laboratory fluorometers are stationary instruments used for more precise and detailed measurements of chlorophyll fluorescence in controlled laboratory settings. They are commonly used for scientific studies and experiments requiring higher accuracy and reproducibility. Both types of fluorometers play important roles in studying and understanding plant physiology and photosynthesis.</p></p>
