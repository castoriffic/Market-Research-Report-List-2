<p><p>Terpene resins are classified into two main types: liquid type and solid type. The liquid type terpene resins consist of highly viscous liquids that are soluble in various organic solvents. They are commonly used as additives in paints, coatings, and adhesives due to their excellent film-forming properties. On the other hand, solid type terpene resins are in solid or semi-solid form and are widely utilized as tackifiers in adhesives and as modifiers in various plastic and rubber products. These resins enhance the adhesive properties and improve the tackiness and durability of the final products.</p></p>
