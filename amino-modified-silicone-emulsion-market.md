<p><p>The amino-modified silicone emulsion market is categorized into three types based on its viscosity levels. Low viscosity emulsion has a relatively thin consistency, providing easy spreading on the surfaces it is applied to. Medium and high viscosity emulsions have a thicker consistency, suitable for applications requiring moderate to high film-forming properties. Ultra-high viscosity emulsion is the thickest among the three types, offering excellent coating and adhesive characteristics. These different viscosity options enable users to choose the most appropriate silicone emulsion based on their specific requirements for surface treatment and protection.</p></p>
