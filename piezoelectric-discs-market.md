<p><p>The piezoelectric discs market consists of two main types: Unimorph and Bimorph. Unimorph piezoelectric discs are composed of a single layer that bends when an electric voltage is applied. They are commonly used in applications such as speakers and actuators. Bimorph piezoelectric discs, on the other hand, consist of two layers that bend in response to an applied voltage. They are often used in applications requiring more complex movements, such as medical devices and sensors. Both types play a crucial role in various industries for their ability to convert electrical energy into mechanical motion.</p></p>
