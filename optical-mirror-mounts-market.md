<p><strong>The Optical Mirror Mounts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adjustable Mirror Mount</li><li>Fixed Mirror Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical mirror mounts are used to securely hold and adjust mirrors in optical systems. Adjustable mirror mounts allow users to change the position of the mirror easily, providing flexibility in alignment and angle adjustments. These mounts typically feature mechanisms like screws or kinematic designs to ensure precise movements. On the other hand, fixed mirror mounts are designed for stable and immovable mirror positioning. They are commonly used when the mirror's position does not require frequent adjustments or when stability and rigidity are crucial for the optical system's performance.</p></p>
