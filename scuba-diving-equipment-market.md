<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Scuba Diving Equipment market is categorized into two types based on respiratory systems - Open Respiratory System and Closed Respiratory System. In the Open Respiratory System, the diver breathes directly from the surrounding water through a regulator attached to the tanks. This system allows for continuous airflow and easy gas exchange. In the Closed Respiratory System, the diver carries a supply of compressed gas in a rebreather device, where exhaled gas is recycled and purified for reuse. This system offers longer dive times and reduced gas consumption.</p></p>
