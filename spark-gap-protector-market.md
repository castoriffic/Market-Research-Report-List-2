<p><p>A spark gap protector, also known as a spark gap arrester or lightning arrester, is a device used to protect electrical systems from voltage surges, such as those caused by lightning strikes or power fluctuations. It works by providing a low-resistance path for the surge current to ground, thereby preventing damage to sensitive equipment and reducing the risk of fire.</p><p>The spark gap protector market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.50% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for effective surge protection solutions in various industries, including power generation, telecommunications, and automotive, is driving the market growth. Manufacturers are investing in research and development activities to enhance the performance of these devices and make them more reliable in harsh environments.</p><p>Additionally, there is a growing trend towards the miniaturization of spark gap protectors, especially in industries where space is a constraint.
