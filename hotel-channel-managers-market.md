<p><p>Hotel channel managers are software solutions that help hotels manage their online distribution channels, such as online travel agencies (OTAs) and global distribution systems (GDSs). Cloud-based channel managers are software platforms accessed via the internet, providing flexibility and scalability without the need for physical servers. On-premises channel managers, on the other hand, are installed directly on a hotel's computer system and require physical servers and on-site maintenance. Both types of channel managers aim to streamline hotel operations and improve distribution efficiency, but differ in terms of accessibility and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
