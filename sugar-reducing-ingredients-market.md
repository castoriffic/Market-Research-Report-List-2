<p><p>The sugar reducing ingredients market can be classified into four main types: sweeteners, bulking agents, texturants, and others. Sweeteners are substances that provide a sweet taste without adding calories or raising blood sugar levels, such as stevia or artificial sweeteners. Bulking agents add volume or texture to food products without adding calories, like polydextrose or cellulose. Texturants improve the texture and mouthfeel of sugar reduced foods, for example, modified starches or gums. The "others" category includes various ingredients used in sugar reduction, such as enzymes, flavors, or natural extracts.</p></p>
