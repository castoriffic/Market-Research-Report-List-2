<p><p>Raw NAND is a type of non-volatile flash memory technology widely used in electronic devices for data storage. The rapid development of 3D NAND technology, which stacks memory cells vertically to increase storage density, has significantly improved the capabilities of raw NAND.</p><p>The market for raw NAND is also witnessing several latest trends.
