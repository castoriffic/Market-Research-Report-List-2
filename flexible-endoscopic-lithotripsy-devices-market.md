<p><p>Flexible endoscopic lithotripsy devices are used for breaking down kidney or bladder stones within the body. Ultrasonic lithotripsy devices use high-frequency sound waves to break stones into smaller fragments, which can be easily passed out. Ballistic lithotripsy devices generate high-pressure shock waves to break stones, and further fragments can be removed using a suctioning probe. Other types of lithotripsy devices include electrohydraulic and laser lithotripsy. These devices offer minimally invasive procedures for stone removal, reducing the need for open surgeries and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
