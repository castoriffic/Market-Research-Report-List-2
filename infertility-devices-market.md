<p><p>Infertility devices market types include various tools and systems used in reproductive medicine. Cryosystems provide freezing and storage solutions for sperm, eggs, or embryos. Ovum aspiration pumps enable the retrieval of eggs during assisted reproductive techniques. Micromanipulators aid in the delicate handling of sperm or embryos for procedures like intracytoplasmic sperm injection. Imaging systems enable the visualization and analysis of reproductive cells and tissues. Incubators provide a controlled environment for the growth of embryos or gametes. Other devices encompass a range of additional tools and technologies used in infertility treatment.</p></p>
