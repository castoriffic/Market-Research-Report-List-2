<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The EMG (Electromyography) Surface Biosensors market consists of two types: Signal Intensity Registration and Pattern Recognition. Signal Intensity Registration involves measuring the strength of muscle contractions through electrodes placed on the skin. It provides quantitative data on muscle activity. On the other hand, Pattern Recognition focuses on interpreting electrical signals from muscles to recognize specific patterns that represent different movements or gestures. This allows for more advanced applications, such as prosthetic control systems or virtual reality devices. Both types of biosensors play a vital role in various fields like sports, healthcare, and robotics.</p></p>
