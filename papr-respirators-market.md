<p><p>PAPR (Powered Air-Purifying Respirators) are protective devices designed to provide a constant flow of filtered air to the wearer. These respirators consist of a headgear and a battery-powered blower unit that pushes filtered air through a hose into a full-face mask or a hood, ensuring that the wearer breathes clean air. PAPR respirators offer improved comfort and extended wearing time compared to traditional respirators, as well as higher levels of protection against airborne pollutants.</p><p>The market for PAPR respirators has seen significant growth in recent years due to increasing concerns over worker safety, particularly in industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. The COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the demand for PAPR respirators, with healthcare workers requiring enhanced protection against infectious diseases.</p><p>Market analysis suggests that the global PAPR respirators market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period.
