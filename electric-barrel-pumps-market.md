<p><p>Electric barrel pumps are available in two main types: steel and plastic. Steel barrel pumps are constructed using robust and durable materials, making them suitable for handling chemicals, oils, and other corrosive liquids. They are designed to withstand harsh environments and provide long-lasting performance.
