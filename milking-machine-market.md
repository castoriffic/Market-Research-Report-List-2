<p><p>The milking machine market consists of two types: fully automatic milking machines and semi-automatic milking machines. Fully automatic machines are designed to perform all aspects of milking without human intervention. They are equipped with sensors and software to accurately monitor and control the milking process. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some human involvement. They typically require operators to attach and detach milking cups manually, while the rest of the process is automated. Both types of machines are used in the dairy industry to increase efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
