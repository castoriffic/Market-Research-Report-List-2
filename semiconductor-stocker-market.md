<p><p>In the semiconductor industry, stocker market types refer to the level of automation in the handling of semiconductor wafers. Semi-automatic stocker systems involve some human intervention, where operators manually load and unload wafers. Fully-automatic stocker systems, on the other hand, require no human intervention and operate with minimal or no manual handling. These systems are capable of loading, unloading, storing, and transferring wafers using robotic arms and advanced software control. The level of automation in stocker systems affects efficiency, accuracy, and reduces the risk of human errors in semiconductor wafer management.</p></p>
