<p><p>A sludge separator is a device used to separate solid waste from liquids. There are two main market types: vertical and horizontal separators. Vertical separators are designed for smaller spaces and operate with a vertical orientation, using gravity to separate the sludge. Horizontal separators are used in larger areas and have a horizontal orientation. These separators use centrifugal force to separate the sludge. Both types serve the same purpose of efficiently separating solid waste from liquids, but their design and operation differ based on the available space and desired process.</p></p>
