<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The LTE Pico Base Station market consists of two types: Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD). TDD technology enables transmission and reception to occur in the same frequency band but at different time intervals. This makes it useful when traffic is unevenly distributed. On the other hand, FDD technology uses separate frequency bands for transmission and reception, allowing simultaneous communication in both directions. FDD is more suitable for balanced traffic scenarios. Both TDD and FDD technologies play a crucial role in meeting the diverse requirements of the LTE Pico Base Station market.</p></p>
