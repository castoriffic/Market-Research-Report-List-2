Wind resistance rowing machines use a flywheel mechanism to generate resistance, simulating the feel of rowing against the wind. Water resistance rowing machines, on the other hand, use paddles immersed in water to create resistance, providing a more realistic rowing experience. Both types of machines offer a challenging cardiovascular workout and are popular choices for individuals looking to improve their overall fitness.</p></p>
