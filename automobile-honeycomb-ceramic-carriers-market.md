<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Automobile honeycomb ceramic carriers are used in automotive exhaust systems to trap and reduce harmful emissions. There are two main types of carriers: straight-through carriers and wall-flow carriers. Straight-through carriers allow exhaust gases to pass through the channels, whereas wall-flow carriers force the gases to flow through porous walls, trapping particulate matter. Straight-through carriers provide lower backpressure and are suitable for vehicles with lower emission levels, while wall-flow carriers are ideal for vehicles requiring higher emission reduction, as they provide better filtration capabilities.</p></p>
