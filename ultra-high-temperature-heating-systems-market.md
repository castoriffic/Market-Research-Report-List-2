<p><p>Ultra High Temperature (UHT) heating systems are used for rapidly heating liquids in various industries. The market can be classified into two types: direct heating and indirect heating. In direct heating, the liquid is heated directly by passing through the UHT system, resulting in a quick and efficient heating process. On the other hand, indirect heating involves the use of a heat exchanger, where the heat is transferred from a heat source to the liquid, ensuring a safe and controlled heating process. These systems find applications in the food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries.</p></p>
