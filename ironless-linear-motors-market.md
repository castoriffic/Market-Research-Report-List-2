<p><p>Ironless linear motors have three main market types: flat type, U-channel type, and tubular type. In the flat type market, the motor consists of a flat coil track and a magnet track, allowing for high force density and a compact design. The U-channel type market involves a U-shaped coil track and a magnet track, providing high force and acceleration capabilities. Lastly, the tubular type market utilizes a tubular coil track and a magnet track, enabling high power density and a cylindrical shape for compact designs.</p></p>
