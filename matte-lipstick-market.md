<p><p>Matte lipstick is categorized into two main types: cream type and fluid type. Cream type matte lipsticks have a thick and creamy texture that provides high coverage and intense color payoff. They are long-lasting and generally have a slightly moisturizing formula. Fluid type matte lipsticks, on the other hand, have a lightweight and liquid-like consistency. They glide smoothly on the lips, providing a more natural finish while still offering long-lasting wear. Both types are popular in the market and cater to different preferences and needs of consumers.</p></p>
