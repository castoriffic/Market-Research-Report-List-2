<p><ul><li>Wet Shotcrete</li><li>Dry Shotcrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for automated shotcrete machines can be categorized into two types: wet shotcrete and dry shotcrete. Wet shotcrete involves mixing water with the concrete mixture before it is sprayed onto the desired surface using a nozzle. This method allows for better control of the mix consistency and is commonly used for applications requiring high strength. Conversely, dry shotcrete involves using pre-mixed dry ingredients that are fed into the machine, where water is added just before the mixture is sprayed. Dry shotcrete is often preferred for applications where time is limited or accessibility is challenging.</p></p>
