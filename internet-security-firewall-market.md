<p><p>The internet security firewall market includes two major types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based firewalls utilize a network of remote servers to store, manage, and protect data, offering scalability and flexibility. On the other hand, on-premise firewalls are physically installed within an organization's infrastructure, allowing for direct control and customization. Both types aim to safeguard networks and systems from malicious threats, but their deployment methods and benefits differ. Cloud-based firewalls are popular for their remote accessibility, while on-premise firewalls are preferred for enhanced control over security measures.</p></p>
