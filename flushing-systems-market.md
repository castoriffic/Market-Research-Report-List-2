<p><p>Flushing systems are categorized into two types: automatic and manual. Automatic flushing systems are designed to automatically flush the toilet after its use, typically utilizing motion sensors or timers. This type of system ensures proper hygiene and saves water by eliminating human error or negligence. On the other hand, manual flushing systems require users to manually activate the flush mechanism, usually through a handle or button. These systems provide more control over water usage but rely on individuals to remember to flush, which may result in hygiene concerns if neglected.</p></p>
