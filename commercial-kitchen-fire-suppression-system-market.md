<p><p>The Commercial Kitchen Fire Suppression System market comprises two main types of systems: Single Agent Fire Suppression System and Dual Agent Fire Suppression System. Single Agent Systems use a specific suppression agent, such as wet or dry chemicals, to extinguish fires. These systems are suitable for smaller kitchens. Dual Agent Systems, on the other hand, combine a liquid fire suppressant with a dry chemical agent to quickly and efficiently control fires in larger commercial kitchens. These systems are designed to provide effective fire protection for a wide range of kitchen equipment.</p></p>
