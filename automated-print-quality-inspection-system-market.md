<p><strong>The Automated Print Quality Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line Print Quality Inspection System</li><li>Off-line Print Quality Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for Automated Print Quality Inspection Systems can be divided into two types: In-line Print Quality Inspection System and Off-line Print Quality Inspection System. </p><p>In-line Print Quality Inspection Systems are integrated into the printing process, allowing real-time monitoring and inspection of print quality as the printing is happening. This helps in identifying and addressing any defects or issues immediately, ensuring high-quality prints. </p><p>Off-line Print Quality Inspection Systems, on the other hand, inspect the print quality after the printing process is completed. This allows for efficient and detailed analysis of the final prints, enabling manufacturers to identify and rectify any quality issues before delivering the products to customers.</p></p>
