<p><p>Compartment interior coating is a protective layer applied on different surfaces inside compartments. Basecoat refers to the initial layer that is applied directly onto the surface to provide adhesion and enhance the durability of the coating system. It acts as a foundation for the subsequent layers. On the other hand, topcoat is the final layer of compartment interior coating that gives the desired appearance and provides ultimate protection against wear, abrasion, chemicals, and UV radiation. It provides a decorative finish and enhances the overall aesthetics of the compartment.</p></p>
