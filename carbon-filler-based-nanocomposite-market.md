<p><p>Carbon filler based nanocomposites are a type of material that consists of a polymer matrix filled with carbon-based nanoparticles. These nanocomposites exhibit enhanced mechanical, thermal, and electrical properties compared to pure polymer composites. With advancements in nanotechnology and increasing demand for lightweight and high-performance materials, there is a growing interest in carbon filler based nanocomposites across various industries. These nanocomposites find applications in automotive, aerospace, electronics, energy, and consumer goods sectors.</p><p>In the automotive industry, carbon filler based nanocomposites are used to reduce the weight of vehicles, leading to improved fuel efficiency.
