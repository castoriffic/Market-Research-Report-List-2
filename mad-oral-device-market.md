<p><p>The MAD (Mandibular Advancement Device) Oral Device is a dental appliance used for the treatment of sleep-disordered breathing conditions, such as snoring and obstructive sleep apnea (OSA). It is designed to keep the airway open during sleep by positioning the lower jaw slightly forward, which helps prevent the collapse of the tongue and soft tissues that obstruct the airway. MAD oral devices are custom-made for each individual patient and are prescribed by sleep specialists or dentists.</p><p>The global MAD Oral Device market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. Factors driving the market growth include the rising prevalence of sleep-disordered breathing conditions, increasing awareness about the health risks associated with untreated sleep apnea, and advancements in MAD oral device technology.</p><p>Moreover, the growing focus on non-invasive treatment options for sleep apnea, in comparison to traditional continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP) therapy, has also fueled the demand for MAD oral devices.
