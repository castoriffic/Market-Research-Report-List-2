<p><strong>The Badge Card Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet</li><li>Dye Sub</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Badge card printing machines are available in various types, including inkjet and dye sublimation (dye sub). Inkjet badge card printers utilize the mechanism of spraying tiny droplets of ink onto the card's surface. This type is commonly chosen for its versatility, allowing color customization and high-resolution printing. On the other hand, dye sub badge card printers apply heat to transfer dye onto the card, resulting in vibrant and durable prints. Dye sub printers are often preferred for professional applications that require long-lasting, scratch-resistant, and water-resistant cards.</p></p>
