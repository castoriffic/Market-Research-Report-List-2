<p><p>The Fingerprint Module market consists of various types such as Optical Fingerprint Module, Silicon Fingerprint Module, and Capacitive Thin Film Transistor (TFT) Fingerprint Module. The Optical Fingerprint Module employs optical scanning technology to capture fingerprint images for identification purposes. Silicon Fingerprint Module, on the other hand, utilizes silicon sensing technology to accurately identify fingerprints. Capacitive TFT Fingerprint Module works by detecting the variations in capacitance caused by the ridges and valleys of a fingerprint. These different types cater to diverse requirements, offering a range of solutions for secure fingerprint recognition.</p></p>
