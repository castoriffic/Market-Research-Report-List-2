<p><p>Polystyrene (PS) resins are a type of plastic used in various applications. General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) is a versatile and transparent form of PS widely used in packaging, consumer goods, and electrical appliances. It offers good stiffness and cost-effectiveness. High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) is a modified version of GPPS, reinforced with rubber or butadiene. It provides superior impact resistance, making it ideal for applications like protective packaging, toys, and disposable tableware. Both GPPS and HIPS resins play significant roles in the PS market, catering to different requirements and industries.</p></p>
