Epoxy-based adhesives provide excellent chemical resistance and are used in demanding environments. Silicone-based adhesives are known for their flexibility and high temperature resistance. Lastly, polyurethane-based adhesives offer good adhesion and are commonly used in the automotive industry. Each type has its unique properties and applications within the SMT adhesives market.</p></p>
