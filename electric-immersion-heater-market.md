<p><p>The electric immersion heater market includes various types of heaters that are designed to be immersed in a liquid to heat it. Immersion heaters are the most common type, consisting of a heating element encased in a tube. Screw plug immersion heaters have a threaded fitting for easy installation. Flanged immersion heaters have a flange for secure attachment to a vessel. Over-the-side immersion heaters hang over the side of a tank while heating it. Lastly, other types of immersion heaters include circulation heaters, pipe heaters, and circulation base heaters.</p></p>
