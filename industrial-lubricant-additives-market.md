<p><ul><li>Additive Package</li><li>Single Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial lubricant additives market consists of two main types: additive package and single component. Additive package refers to a combination of various additives that are blended together to enhance the performance of lubricants. These additives can include anti-wear agents, rust inhibitors, and viscosity index improvers, among others. On the other hand, single component additives are individual substances added to lubricants to improve specific properties, such as friction reduction or extreme pressure protection. Both additive packages and single component additives play a crucial role in enhancing the effectiveness and durability of industrial lubricants.</p></p>
