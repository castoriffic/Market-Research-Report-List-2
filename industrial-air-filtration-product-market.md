<p><p>The industrial air filtration product market consists of various types of filters designed to remove contaminants from the air. High Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters are known for their ability to capture small particles effectively. Ultra-Low Particulate Air (ULPA) filters are similar to HEPA filters but have a higher efficiency in removing smaller particles. Bag filters use a fabric bag to trap dust and pollutants. Electrostatic precipitators use an electrical charge to attract and remove particles. Other types of filters may include carbon filters, deep bed filters, and electrostatic filters.</p></p>
