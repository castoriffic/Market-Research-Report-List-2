<p><p>E-methanol, also known as electro-methanol or renewable methanol, is a carbon-neutral fuel derived from carbon dioxide (CO2) and hydrogen. E-methanol has gained attention as a potential alternative to conventional methanol due to its lower greenhouse gas emissions and potential for reducing dependence on fossil fuels.</p><p>The E-methanol market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
