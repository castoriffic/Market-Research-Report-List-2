There are three types of dearomatic solvents based on their flash points - low, medium, and high. Low flash point de-aromatic solvents have the lowest ignition point and are more volatile, making them suitable for applications requiring fast evaporation. Medium flash point de-aromatic solvents have a moderate ignition point and are commonly used in industries where balance between volatility and safety is required. High flash point de-aromatic solvents have the highest ignition point and are used in applications where safety is a top concern.</p></p>
