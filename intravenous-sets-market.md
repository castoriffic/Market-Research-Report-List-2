<p><p>Intravenous (IV) sets are medical devices used to deliver fluids, medications, or nutrients directly into the veins of patients. IV sets are widely used in hospitals, clinics, and home care settings for various therapeutic purposes.</p><p>The global intravenous sets market is expected to witness significant growth over the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, such as diabetes and cancer, coupled with the growing geriatric population, is driving the demand for advanced medical technologies, including IV sets. Advancements in IV administration techniques, such as needle-free connectors and safety infusion systems, are further propelling the market growth.</p><p>Another key driver for market growth is the rising number of surgical procedures worldwide. IV sets are extensively used during surgeries for fluid and medication administration. The growing volume of surgical procedures, especially in emerging economies, contributes to the market expansion.</p><p>Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has also positively influenced the demand for IV sets.
