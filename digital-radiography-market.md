<p><p>Digital Radiography is a type of X-ray imaging where digital X-ray sensors are used in place of traditional photographic film. <p><strong>The Digital Radiography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CR Tech Digital X-Ray System market refers to the market for computed radiography (CR) technology in digital X-ray systems. CR technology uses imaging plates to capture X-ray images, which are then converted into digital format for viewing and analysis. </p><p>On the other hand, the DR Tech Digital X-Ray System market pertains to the market for direct radiography (DR) technology in digital X-ray systems. DR technology directly captures X-ray images using digital detectors, eliminating the need for imaging plates.</p><p>Both markets focus on digital X-ray systems but differ in the technology used for image capture and conversion, with CR utilizing imaging plates and DR using digital detectors.</p></p>
