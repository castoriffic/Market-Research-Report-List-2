<p><p>The DNV Certified Offshore Container market encompasses various types of containers designed for offshore applications. Half height containers have a lower height and are used for transporting heavy goods or goods that require top-loading. Open top containers have an open roof, allowing for easy loading of oversized or heavy items. Baskets are specialized containers used for lifting and transferring materials.
