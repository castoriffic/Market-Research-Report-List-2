<p><p>Smart electrical meters are devices used to measure and monitor the consumption of electricity in households and businesses. They are available in two main types: single-phase and three-phase meters. Single-phase meters are typically used in residential buildings and small businesses as they measure the electrical consumption of appliances and lighting systems running on a single phase. Three-phase meters, on the other hand, are commonly used in larger commercial and industrial settings where high-power equipment and machinery operate on three-phase electrical systems. Both types of meters enable accurate billing and provide real-time data for better energy management.</p></p>
