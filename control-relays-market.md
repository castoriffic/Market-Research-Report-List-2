<p><ul><li>Impulse Relays</li><li>Monitoring Relays</li><li>Signal Conditioning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The control relays market consists of various types including impulse relays, monitoring relays, and signal conditioning relays. Impulse relays are used to control an electrical circuit with a momentary pulse of current, while monitoring relays are designed to monitor specific electrical parameters and initiate an appropriate response if they fall outside the predetermined range. Signal conditioning relays are used to convert and condition electrical signals to ensure compatibility between different systems. These relays play a crucial role in controlling and monitoring electrical circuits in various industries.</p></p>
