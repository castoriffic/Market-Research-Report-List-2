<p><p>The Automatic License Plate Recognition (ALPR) market consists of two main types: Fixed and Mobile. Mobile ALPR systems, on the other hand, are mounted on vehicles and are designed to scan and capture license plate information while in motion, enabling law enforcement agencies or parking authorities to identify and track vehicles in real-time.
