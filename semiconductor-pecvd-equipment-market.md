<p><strong>The Semiconductor PECVD Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anode PECVD System</li><li>Cathode PECVD System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment market comprises two main types: Anode PECVD system and Cathode PECVD system. Anode PECVD system refers to a deposition method where a positively charged electrode or anode is used to generate plasma, creating a high-energy environment for film deposition onto the semiconductor substrate. On the other hand, Cathode PECVD system involves the use of a negatively charged electrode or cathode to generate plasma for film deposition. Both systems are widely used in the semiconductor industry to fabricate thin films with precise thickness and composition on various substrates.</p></p>
