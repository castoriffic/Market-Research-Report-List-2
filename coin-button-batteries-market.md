<p><p>Coin button batteries are classified into various types based on the materials used in their construction. LR batteries, also known as alkaline batteries, utilize an alkaline electrolyte and are commonly used in low-drain devices like watches. SR batteries, or silver oxide batteries, employ a silver oxide electrolyte and are suitable for high-drain devices such as calculators and cameras. CR batteries, or lithium batteries, employ a lithium-based electrolyte and excel in high-drain applications like fitness trackers. Apart from these three major categories, there are other less common types of coin button batteries available in the market.</p></p>
