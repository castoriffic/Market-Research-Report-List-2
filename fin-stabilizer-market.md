<p><p>Fin stabilizers are devices used on ships to minimize roll motion in rough sea conditions. There are two main types of fin stabilizers in the market: retractable and non-retractable. Retractable fin stabilizers can be extended or retracted as needed, providing flexibility in operation. They are suitable for larger vessels and offer higher efficiency. On the other hand, non-retractable fin stabilizers remain fixed in position and are typically used in smaller vessels. They are less complex and require less maintenance but may have lower efficiency compared to retractable ones.</p></p>
