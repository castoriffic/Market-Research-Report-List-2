<p><p>The Windshield Head-up Display (W-HUD) market can be categorized into two types: front mounting and rear mounting. Rear mounting W-HUD, on the other hand, involves mounting the display unit on the rearview mirror, projecting essential information onto the windshield, allowing the driver to view it without distraction.</p></p>
