Diagnostic screening involves identifying genetic variations associated with diseases or conditions in individuals. PGD is a process used in reproductive medicine to detect genetic abnormalities in embryos before implantation. Relationship testing determines biological parentage or kinship by analyzing genetic markers. These different market types of genetic sequencing aim to provide individuals with valuable insights into their health, reproductive choices, and familial connections.</p></p>
