<p><p>Tray sealing machines are categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require human intervention in each step of the sealing process, such as placing trays and applying the seal. Semi-automatic machines partially automate the process, with some steps being carried out automatically, while others require manual input. Fully automatic machines are entirely automated, requiring minimal human intervention, as they perform all steps of the sealing process automatically. These different types cater to varying production requirements and offer different levels of efficiency and convenience in packaging operations.</p></p>
