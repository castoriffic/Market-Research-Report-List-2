<p><p>The unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) system market encompasses various types of UAVs. Fixed-wing UAVs have fixed wings like an airplane, enabling long-range flights and efficient surveillance. Rotary wing UAVs, such as helicopters, provide versatility in terms of takeoff and landing, making them suitable for various operations. Flapping-wing UAVs mimic the flight of birds or insects, facilitating maneuverability in narrow spaces. Airship UAVs use lighter-than-air technology, offering stability and prolonged airborne missions. Each type finds applications in diverse fields like military, agriculture, transportation, and surveillance, catering to the specific requirements of different industries.</p></p>
