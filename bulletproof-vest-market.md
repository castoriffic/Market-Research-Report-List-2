Soft bulletproof vests are made with materials like Kevlar and offer flexibility and comfort while providing protection against handgun ammunition. Hard bulletproof vests are made with rigid materials like ceramic or metal plates and are highly effective against rifle ammunition. Composite bulletproof vests combine the qualities of both soft and hard vests, utilizing a combination of soft armor panels and hard plates to offer enhanced protection.
