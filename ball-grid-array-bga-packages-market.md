<p><p>Ball Grid Array (BGA) packages are used in electronic devices for mounting integrated circuits on the circuit boards. There are several types of BGA packages in the market. The Molded Array Process BGA involves encapsulating the package with a mold compound, providing protection and improving reliability. Thermally Enhanced BGA packages have additional features such as heat sinks or die attach pads to enhance thermal performance. Package on Package (PoP) BGA allows stacking of multiple chips vertically, increasing the functionality without expanding the PCB footprint. Micro BGA packages are smaller in size, enabling higher-density circuit integration.</p></p>
