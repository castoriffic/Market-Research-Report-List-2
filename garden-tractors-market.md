<p><p>Garden tractors market consists of two main types: wheeled tractors and crawler tractors. Wheeled tractors are equipped with wheels and are commonly used for lighter tasks in gardens and lawns. They are generally more maneuverable and easier to handle. Crawler tractors, on the other hand, are fitted with tracks instead of wheels, providing better traction and stability. This makes them suitable for heavy-duty tasks in rough or uneven terrains. Both types of tractors serve the purpose of aiding in gardening and landscaping, but they differ in terms of mobility and efficiency in different environments.</p></p>
