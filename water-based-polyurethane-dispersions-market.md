<p><strong>The Water-Based Polyurethane Dispersions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-component PUD</li><li>One-component PUD</li><li>Urethane-modified</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-Based Polyurethane Dispersions (PUD) are widely used in various industries due to their excellent properties like durability, flexibility, and resistance to chemicals. There are three main types of PUD in the market:</p><p>1. Two-component PUD: This type requires the mixing of two components, usually a polyol and an isocyanate, before application. It offers a high level of durability and is commonly used in industries where superior performance is required.</p><p>2. One-component PUD: Unlike the two-component PUD, this type is ready-to-use and does not require any additional mixing. It is easy to handle and cost-effective, making it suitable for applications where faster and simpler processes are desired.</p><p>3. Urethane-modified PUD: This type combines the properties of both polyurethane and other polymers to enhance specific characteristics. It offers improved water resistance, adhesion, and elasticity compared to traditional PUDs. Urethane-modified PUDs are particularly useful in applications that demand high performance and durability.</p></p>
