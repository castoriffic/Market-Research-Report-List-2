<p><p>The mushroom powder market can be divided into two types: organic and non-organic. Organic mushroom powder is produced from mushrooms that are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms. It is considered healthier and more environmentally friendly due to its natural cultivation methods. On the other hand, non-organic mushroom powder is produced from conventionally grown mushrooms that may have been exposed to synthetic chemicals and genetically modified practices. While non-organic mushroom powder may be more readily available and cheaper, it may contain residues of these substances and may not be as beneficial for health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
