<p><p>Ophthalmic ultrasound imaging systems are used for diagnosing and monitoring eye conditions and diseases. The market includes various types of ultrasound scans such as A-scan, B-scan, combined scan, pachymeter, and ultrasound biomicroscopy (UBM). A-scan measures the length of the eye, B-scan provides images of the eye's interior, combined scan integrates A-scan and B-scan capabilities, pachymeter measures corneal thickness, and UBM offers high-resolution imaging of the anterior segment of the eye. These systems play a crucial role in ophthalmology by facilitating accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
