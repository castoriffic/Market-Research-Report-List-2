<p><p>A shell and tube cooler is a type of heat exchanger used to cool fluids or gases in various industries. It consists of a shell, which houses a bundle of tubes where the cooling fluid flows. The fluid or gas to be cooled passes through the tube side, while the cooling fluid surrounds the tubes in the shell side, facilitating heat transfer.</p><p>The current outlook of the shell and tube cooler market is positive, with a steady growth rate expected in the coming years.
