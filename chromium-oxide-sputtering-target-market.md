The market for these targets can be categorized into two types: rotary type and non-rotatable type. </p><p>1. Rotary type: This market segment includes sputtering targets that rotate during the deposition process. They are often used for large area coating applications and can provide uniform film thickness distribution.</p><p>2. Non-rotatable type: This market segment comprises sputtering targets that do not rotate during the deposition process. They are commonly used in small area coating applications and are suitable for achieving high film quality and precision in deposition.</p><p>Both types cater to different industry requirements and provide solutions for various thin film coating needs.</p></p>
