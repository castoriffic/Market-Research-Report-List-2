<p><p>Non-alcoholic malt beverages are classified into three main types: dairy-based, carbonated, and soda drinks. Dairy-based malt beverages are made from malted grains and combined with milk or dairy products to create a creamy and smooth texture. Carbonated malt beverages have carbon dioxide added to them, resulting in a fizzy and refreshing taste. Soda malt drinks are combined with extracts and flavors to mimic the taste of popular soda beverages, providing a non-alcoholic alternative. These three types cater to different consumer preferences for taste, texture, and carbonation levels.</p></p>
