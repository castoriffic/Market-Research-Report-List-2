<p><p>Plant Extract Hair Dye market consists of two major types: Permanent Hair Dye and Semi-Permanent Hair Dye. Permanent Hair Dye provides long-lasting color by penetrating the hair shaft and altering its natural pigment, resulting in a permanent color change. It requires regular touch-ups as the hair grows. On the other hand, Semi-Permanent Hair Dye coats the hair shaft with color that gradually fades over time. It does not penetrate the hair shaft, making it less damaging and allowing for more flexibility in color changes.</p></p>
