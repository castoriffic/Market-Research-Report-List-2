<p><p>Failure Analysis Equipment Market is segmented into four types: Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM), Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM), Focused Ion Beam (FIB), and Dual Beam. SEM delivers high-resolution images of the surface, while TEM enables analysis of internal structures at atomic levels. FIB is used for milling, deposition, and imaging at the nanoscale. Dual Beam combines the capabilities of FIB and SEM, allowing for simultaneous imaging and cross-sectioning. These equipment are crucial in identifying the root cause of failures, assisting in industries such as electronics, automotive, and semiconductors.</p></p>
