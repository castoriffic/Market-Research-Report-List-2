<p><p>The Flat Panel Display Equipment market consists of various types such as Evaporation, Photolithography, Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD), Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA), Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD), and Wet Etch. Evaporation involves the process of vaporizing a material and depositing it on a substrate to create a thin film. Photolithography is used for exposing patterns onto a substrate using light. PVD is a method of depositing materials onto a surface through physical processes. ELA uses high-intensity ultraviolet light to enhance the properties of thin films. CVD involves the deposition of materials through chemical reactions. Wet Etch is a process of removing material layers from a substrate using chemical solutions.</p></p>
