<p><p>A baby sling is a type of carrier that allows parents to comfortably carry their babies while keeping their hands free. Baby slings offer various benefits, including increased bonding between parent and baby, ease of breastfeeding, and convenience for parents.</p><p>The baby sling market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory.
