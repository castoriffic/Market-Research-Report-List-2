<p><p>Angle sensors are devices used to measure the angle or position of an object. TMR (Tunneling Magnetoresistance) sensors are based on the principle of electron tunneling and provide high accuracy, low power consumption, and resistance to external magnetic fields. Hall Effect sensors utilize the Hall Effect phenomenon to detect changes in magnetic fields and convert them into electrical signals. They offer long lifespan and high reliability. Other types in the market include optical, inductive, capacitive, and potentiometric sensors, each with their own unique operating principles and applications.</p></p>
