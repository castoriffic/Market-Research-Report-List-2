<p><strong>The ECG Telemetry Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>ECG telemetry equipment is used to monitor and record the electrical activity of the heart. The market for ECG telemetry equipment can be categorized into three types. </p><p>Resting ECG devices are used to measure the heart's electrical activity when the patient is at rest. These devices are commonly used for routine check-ups and diagnosis of heart-related conditions.</p><p>Stress ECG devices, also known as exercise ECG or treadmill ECG devices, are used to measure the heart's activity during physical exertion or stress. These devices help doctors assess the heart's performance and detect any abnormalities that may arise during exercise.</p><p>The "other" market includes additional types of ECG telemetry equipment such as Holter monitors, event recorders, and implantable loop recorders. Holter monitors record the heart's activity continuously over a certain period, while event recorders are used for intermittent monitoring. Implantable loop recorders are devices implanted under the skin to provide long-term monitoring of the heart's electrical activity.</p><p>In summary, the ECG telemetry equipment market consists of resting ECG devices, stress ECG devices, and various other devices used for different monitoring purposes.</p></p>
