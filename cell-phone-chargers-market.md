<p><p>Cell phone chargers can be divided into two main market types: wired chargers and wireless chargers. Wired chargers are traditional chargers that require a physical connection between the charger and the phone. They use cables and plug directly into the phone's charging port. On the other hand, wireless chargers offer a cable-free charging experience. They use electromagnetic fields to transfer energy between the charger and the phone, enabling convenient charging by simply placing the phone on a charging pad or stand.</p></p>
