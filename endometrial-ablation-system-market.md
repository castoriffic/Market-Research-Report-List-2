<p><p>The Endometrial Ablation System market consists of various types of procedures for treating excessive uterine bleeding. Electrical or electrocautery involves the use of an electric current to remove the endometrial lining. Hydrothermal ablation utilizes heated liquid to destroy the lining. Balloon therapy employs a heated balloon to ablate the tissue. Radiofrequency ablation applies electrical energy to remove the lining. Cryoablation involves freezing the lining, while microwave ablation utilizes microwaves to destroy the tissue. These different techniques offer options for patients with varying needs and preferences.</p></p>
