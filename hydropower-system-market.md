<p><p>The hydropower system market consists of different types of turbines used to generate power from flowing water. Impulse turbines use the kinetic energy of the water to generate power, typically by splitting the water jet into multiple streams. Reaction turbines, on the other hand, utilize both the kinetic energy and pressure of the water to produce electricity. These turbines are designed differently and have varying applications depending on factors such as water flow, head height, and available resources.</p></p>
