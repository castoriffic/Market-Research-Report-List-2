<p><ul><li>Primary HIP Replacement</li><li>Partial HIP Replacement</li><li>Revision HIP Replacement</li><li>HIP Resurfacing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hip replacement devices market consists of various types, including primary hip replacement, partial hip replacement, revision hip replacement, and hip resurfacing. Primary hip replacement involves replacing the entire hip joint with an artificial prosthesis. Partial hip replacement is the replacement of only the damaged portion of the joint. Revision hip replacement is performed when a previous implant fails or wears out. Hip resurfacing involves capping the damaged hip joint with a metal prosthesis. These different types cater to specific patient needs and conditions, providing a range of solutions for hip joint problems.</p></p>
