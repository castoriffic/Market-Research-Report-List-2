Carbon fiber composites are produced by incorporating carbon fibers into plastic, providing exceptional strength and lightweight properties. The "others" category includes various composites made with different reinforcing materials like natural fibers or aramid fibers. These thermoplastic composites are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction, due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
