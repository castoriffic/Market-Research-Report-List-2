<p><p>The mattress coil market offers various types of coils, including continuous coils, Bonnell coils, offset coils, and Marshall coils. Continuous coils feature one long, continuous wire that provides consistent support. Bonnell coils are hourglass-shaped and interconnected through helical wires for durability. Offset coils have a hinged construction that responds individually to weight and movement. Marshall coils, also known as pocket coils, are encased in separate fabric pockets, reducing motion transfer and enhancing support. These different coil types cater to different consumer preferences in terms of support, motion isolation, and durability.</p></p>
