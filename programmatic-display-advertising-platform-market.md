<p><p>Programmatic display advertising platforms are classified into two types: cloud-based and on-premise market. Cloud-based platforms operate online and are accessible from any location with an internet connection. They offer flexibility, scalability, and require no hardware installation. On the other hand, on-premise platforms are installed on local servers, giving advertisers complete control over their data. However, they require maintenance and can be less flexible than cloud-based solutions. Both market types offer programmatic advertising solutions but differ in terms of accessibility, control, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
