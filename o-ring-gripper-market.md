<p><p>The O-ring gripper market consists of two main types: parallel gripper and angular gripper. Parallel grippers have two jaws that move parallel to each other when gripping objects, making them suitable for handling objects with different sizes. On the other hand, angular grippers have jaws that move towards each other at an angle, allowing them to grip objects from different orientations. Both types of grippers offer versatility in industrial automation, improving efficiency and productivity in various applications.</p></p>
