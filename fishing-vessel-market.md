<p><p>A fishing vessel, also known as a fishing boat or fishing trawler, is a vessel designed for catching fish in the sea or freshwater bodies. Additionally, technological advancements in fishing gear and navigation systems are enhancing the efficiency and productivity of fishing vessels, further boosting market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the adoption of sustainable fishing practices and the implementation of regulations to prevent overfishing are creating opportunities for innovative fishing vessel designs. These designs focus on reducing environmental impact and optimizing fuel efficiency. For instance, there is a growing trend towards the use of hybrid or electric propulsion systems in fishing vessels to reduce fuel consumption and emissions.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a rising demand for larger capacity fishing vessels with advanced onboard processing facilities.
