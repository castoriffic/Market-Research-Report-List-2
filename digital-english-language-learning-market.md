<p><p>There are two main types of digital English language learning markets: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise language learning involves purchasing and installing software on individual computers or servers, allowing learners to access the program without an internet connection. Conversely, cloud-based language learning is accessible online, with learners accessing the software through web browsers or dedicated mobile apps. This cloud-based approach offers greater flexibility, scalability, and collaborative features as learners can access materials and interact with others across different devices and locations.</p></p>
