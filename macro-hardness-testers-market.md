<p><p>The Macro Hardness Testers market can be classified into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and full-automatic. Manual hardness testers require human intervention throughout the testing process, where the operator applies the necessary load and measures the indentation manually. Semi-automatic testers automate the process of applying the load but still require manual indentation measurement. Full-automatic testers automate both load application and indentation measurement, providing a more efficient and accurate testing process. These different types offer varying levels of automation and convenience to cater to the diverse requirements of the industry.</p></p>
