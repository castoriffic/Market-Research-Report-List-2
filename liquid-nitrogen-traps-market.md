<p><strong>The Liquid Nitrogen Traps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline</li><li>Right Angle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid nitrogen traps market consists of two main types: inline and right angle traps. Inline traps are designed to be installed in a straight line along the gas or vacuum line, allowing the gas or vapor to pass through the trap, where the liquid nitrogen cools and condenses any impurities or contaminants. Right angle traps, on the other hand, are installed at a right angle to the gas or vacuum line, providing a more compact solution for trapping and removing impurities. Both types are widely used in various industries for efficient purification processes.</p></p>
