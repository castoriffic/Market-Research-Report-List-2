<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore oil and gas pipeline market is classified into three types: ERW (Electric Resistance Welding) pipes, LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welding) pipes, and SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welding) pipes. ERW pipes are welded using an electrical current, LSAW pipes are welded along the longitudinal direction, and SSAW pipes are welded spirally. These types of pipes are used for the transportation of oil and gas in offshore projects, providing a safe and efficient means of transferring resources from the source to onshore facilities.</p></p>
