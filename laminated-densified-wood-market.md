<p><p>The laminated densified wood market consists of two main types: fully impregnated densified laminated wood and non-impregnated densified laminated wood. Fully impregnated densified laminated wood is treated with a special resin that permeates the entire structure, resulting in increased durability, water resistance, and dimensional stability. Non-impregnated densified laminated wood, on the other hand, lacks this resin treatment and may therefore exhibit lower resistance to moisture and reduced dimensional stability. Both types of laminated densified wood offer unique characteristics and are used in various applications based on their specific properties.</p></p>
