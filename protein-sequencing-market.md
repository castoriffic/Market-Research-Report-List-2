<p><strong>The Protein Sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edman Degradation</li><li>Mass Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protein sequencing is used to determine the amino acid sequence in a protein. The market for protein sequencing includes different types, such as Edman Degradation and Mass Spectrometry. Edman Degradation is a chemical method that involves sequentially removing and identifying amino acids from the protein. Mass Spectrometry, on the other hand, analyzes the mass and charge of the protein fragments generated by breaking the protein apart. Both methods are widely used in the field of proteomics for protein identification, characterization, and structural analysis.</p></p>
