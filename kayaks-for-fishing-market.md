<p><p>Kayaks for fishing come in two main market types: hard body kayaks and inflatable kayaks. Hard body kayaks are made of rigid materials such as plastic or fiberglass, providing stability and durability for rougher waters. They are heavier and require more storage space, but offer better performance and speed. On the other hand, inflatable kayaks are lighter and easier to transport and store. They are made of tough materials and can withstand punctures. Although less stable, they are more affordable and convenient for occasional fishermen or those with limited storage space.</p></p>
