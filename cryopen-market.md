<p><p>CryoPen is a medical device that utilizes cryotherapy, a technique involving the use of extreme cold to treat various skin conditions such as skin tags, warts, moles, and other lesions. This pen-like device emits a precise jet of nitrous oxide to freeze the targeted area, causing the affected cells to die and eventually fall off. CryoPen is an alternative to traditional methods like surgical excision, cryosurgery, or electrocautery, and is known for its effectiveness, quick procedure time, minimal pain, and low risk of scarring.</p><p>The CryoPen Market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of skin conditions and the growing demand for non-invasive treatment options are driving the market growth. Cryotherapy is gaining popularity due to its advantages over conventional surgical procedures. The technique does not require anesthesia, has a shorter recovery time, and avoids the need for sutures, making it a preferred choice for both patients and healthcare providers.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in cryotherapy technology and the introduction of innovative features in CryoPen devices are further fueling market growth.
