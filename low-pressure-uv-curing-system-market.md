<p><strong>The Low Pressure UV Curing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spot Cure</li><li>Flood Cure</li><li>Focused Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low-pressure UV curing systems are used in various applications such as spot cure, flood cure, and focused beam curing. Spot cure refers to the process of curing a specific area or point on a surface using focused UV light. Flood cure, on the other hand, involves the uniform application of UV light across a larger area or surface. Focused beam curing entails the use of a concentrated and intense beam of UV light to cure or dry materials in a precise manner.</p></p>
