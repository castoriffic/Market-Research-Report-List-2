<p><p>The Electric Green Taxiing System (EGTS) is an innovative technology that aims to reduce fuel consumption and emissions during taxiing of aircraft. It enables aircraft to move on the ground using electric motors rather than relying solely on jet engines, thereby saving fuel and reducing carbon emissions. The EGTS utilizes electric motors installed in the main landing gear wheels, which are powered by an onboard electrical system, allowing the aircraft to maneuver on the ground without the need for jet engines.</p><p>The market for Electric Green Taxiing System (EGTS) is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 10.2%. The increasing concerns over environmental pollution and rising fuel costs are driving the demand for more sustainable and fuel-efficient alternatives in the aviation industry. The EGTS technology offers substantial benefits in terms of fuel savings, reduced emissions, and quieter operations, making it an attractive solution for airlines.</p><p>Moreover, the growing emphasis on sustainable aviation and the implementation of stringent environmental regulations by various authorities further propel the adoption of EGTS. Airlines are increasingly investing in retrofitting existing aircraft with EGTS or incorporating them in new-build aircraft to enhance their operational efficiency and reduce their environmental footprint.</p><p>The Electric Green Taxiing System (EGTS) market is witnessing several trends that are influencing its growth. The development of lightweight and compact EGTS units, advancements in electric motor technology, and improved battery efficiency are driving innovation in the industry. Additionally, partnerships between aircraft manufacturers, airlines, and technology providers are fostering research and development activities and facilitating the commercialization of EGTS solutions.</p><p>In conclusion, the Electric Green Taxiing System (EGTS) market is poised for significant growth due to the increasing demand for sustainable aviation solutions. <p><p>The Electric Green Taxiing System (EGTS) market encompasses two main types: on-board type and external systems type. This type typically involves retrofitting existing aircraft with electric taxiing technology. On the other hand, the external systems type market focuses on the establishment of ground-based infrastructure that enables aircraft to be electrically taxiied while still connected to an external power source. Both types aim to reduce fuel consumption and emissions during taxiing operations.</p></p>
