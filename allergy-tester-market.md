<p><p>The allergy tester market consists of various types of testers used to diagnose allergies. The skin prick tester involves applying allergens to the skin and pricking it to check for a reaction. The intradermal tester injects allergens below the skin and observes the response. Blood testers analyze blood samples for allergen-specific antibodies. Other types may include patch tests or provocation tests. Each type serves the purpose of identifying allergic reactions, allowing healthcare professionals to provide accurate diagnoses and treatment plans for patients with allergies.</p></p>
