<p><strong>The Industrial Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-Mount</li><li>CS-Mount</li><li>F-Mount</li><li>S-Mount</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial lenses are optical devices used in various industries for capturing images and videos. There are several types of industrial lenses available in the market. C-Mount lenses have a 1-inch diameter and are commonly used in machine vision applications. CS-Mount lenses have a shorter back focal length, making them suitable for compact cameras. F-Mount lenses are used in high-resolution imaging systems. S-Mount lenses have a small M12 thread and find applications in small form factor cameras. Lastly, there are other types of industrial lenses that are designed for specialized applications, such as telecentric lenses or zoom lenses.</p></p>
