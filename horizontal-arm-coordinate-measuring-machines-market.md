<p><p>Horizontal Arm Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) can be classified into two types: Computer Numerical Control (CNC) and Manually-Controlled. CNC CMMs are operated through computer programming, allowing for automated and precise measurements. They offer advanced features such as multi-axis control, data analysis, and programming flexibility. On the other hand, manually-controlled CMMs require operators to move the arm and probe manually to measure the dimensions of the object. These machines are often more affordable but may lack the precision and efficiency of their CNC counterparts.</p></p>
