<p><p>Tyre testing market includes various types such as strength testing, endurance testing, rolling resistance testing, and others. Strength testing measures the maximum load a tyre can bear before failure. Endurance testing assesses the tyre's performance over time in order to ensure durability. Rolling resistance testing evaluates the energy consumed by the tyre while rolling on the road. Other types of testing may involve examining the tyre's traction, noise generation, and braking capabilities. These tests help manufacturers develop high-quality tyres that meet safety, performance, and efficiency standards.</p></p>
